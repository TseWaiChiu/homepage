3. **Automated Endoscopy Reporting Using a Stream-Oriented Multimodal Large Language Model (EndoScribe): A Multicentre Cohort Study**<br>
   **NEJM AI · Under Review · Student First Author**

   > EndoScribe is a stream-oriented multimodal large language model system for generating standardized endoscopy reports. It combines large-scale medical vision-language data processing, domain-specific SFT and RL, and medical-keyword evaluation to improve report completeness and clinical efficiency.
